# Who's already claiming credits?

<div class="icontain"><iframe width='80%' height='100%' frameBorder='0' src='map.html'></iframe></div>

### Select a Property to get more information.
{: .right}

Click on the supplied link in the upper-right corner to view the tax payment information for the current year.

<br>

Property data was gathered from the [**Baltimore Open GIS Portal**](http://gisdata.baltimorecity.gov/).  We filtered the data to identify all of the owner-occupied homes.    Among these properties, we identified which homeowners were receiving the tax credit and how much they were saving.

<br>

### Some of the things we've found:
{: .left}

#### Owner occupied homes make up **48.3%** of all properties in Baltimore city.
{: .left}

#### Less than **6%** of these homeowners are taking advantage of the state tax credit.
{: .left}
